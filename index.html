Service Offerings

Unbundling the deal lifecycle.

As an AI-native firm, we work with business owners, founders and private equity firms on specific parts of a deal cycle – each representing an outcome.

Pre-Deal
  • Strategic review / deal options
  • Investment story / highlights
  • Business Model Analysis
  • Normalised EBITDA calculation
  • Historical Financial Statements
  • Forecasts and Business Plan
  • Buyers / Targets list
  • Quick valuation (Comps, DCF)
  • Data room index and set-up
Deal
  • Collaterals preparation (Teaser, Information Memorandum, Management Presentation)
  • Detailed bottom-up valuation
  • Due diligence, Q&A
  • Process and Timetable
  • Non-binding and binding transaction documentation
  • Investment execution and negotiations
Post-Deal
  • ESOP / Incentive Plans
  • Budgeting process and templates
  • Board / governance assistance and risk management
  • Investor reporting and alignment
  • Team buildout
  • Joint ventures and new market / product partnerships
